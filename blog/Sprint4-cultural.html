Hi and welcome to my latest Sprint4 cultural blog post. In this post I will be sharing some information about my research on the topic of Neuroplasticity and Growth Mindset, what it is, and why it is relevant

So lets get started, What is Neuroplasticity

Neuroplasticity is the brains ability to expand and grow when learning and changeing. This is because the brain has the ability to change or expand like plastic hence why the name. Simply put Neuroplasticity is when your brain's neural pathways are altered as an effect of environmental, behavioral, and neural change. When you are exposed to new information you brain starts to change and create new pathways. Those pathways expand the more frequently you are exposed to that information.

Neuroplasticity happens continually as you learn and memorize new data. One way we can take advantage of Neuroplasticity while learning is by being exposed to the same information frequently. This can be done in many forms such as reading material memorising or even practising and implementing what you are learning. We can use this principle when trying to learn coding or a new language. The more we code using a specific language the more better we will become at it and it will become easier to store in our brains.

Growth Mindset and what is it

Being able to grow or learn things in life starts of by first requiring a mental change. You need to be in the right type of mindset to be able to grow and adapt and to learn new things what ever they maybe. This is where being in a growth mindset mentally is the key. Having this type of mindset allows for a person to recognise that abilities can be developed over time with effort and consistency. This type of mindset can be developed in all aspects of life. The opposite of this is a fixed mindeset in which a person in that state mentally believes that abilities is a natural reality and that it can not be developed with effort.

I believe in order for me or anyone to be in this type of mindset requires motivation inspiration and a postive attitude. Reason being is you need to have a catalyst for change. You need to have a reason as to why you want to change. There also needs to be a good attitude in order for you to follow up on the expectations you have set for yourself to be able to grow. Having a good attitude will allow you remain consistent on the task at hand. Hopefull you have gotten something out of this blog that you can implement to make a postive change in your life.
