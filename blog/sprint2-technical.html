Differences between Margin, Border, Padding

This page will outline the differences between margin, border andpadding when used in css. We will go through these in steps.

Step1

In order for us to to know the differences and how they are used we must first understand what they are.

  • Margin --Used to create space, can be difined in length or percentage
  • Border --Allows you to specify the style of a borer such as width color or thickness. The propery allows you to confine text within a box
  • Padding --Padding is used to creat or define the space around a peice of wording within any borders

    • Step2

    What are the difference between the three.

    Take a scenario where you need to insert a tab or a button on a website or a catalogue. You will tyep the text and surround it with a border. Then you will you will use the padding element to dictact how much space is in between the text within the border. THen you can use margin to determine where you will palce the tab or button you just created.
