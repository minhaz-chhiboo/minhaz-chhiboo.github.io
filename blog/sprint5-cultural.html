Reflection Foundation Core SKills

Hi and welcome to my Sprint5 cultural blog post. In this post we will review some of the core learnings covered throughout foundations.

What are three new things you have learned about yourself and your ego as a result of the core learning?

    I learnt that I am very bad with manageing time.
    I learnt that I learn best by doing things that I learnt more often.
    I learnt more about my emotional state and emotional intelligence.

What are the role of values, empathy and self awareness in learning and programming?

I believe that self awareness is critical when trying to learn new things. If you are about to be aware of the states you are in you can learn more effectively by changing your state.

What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

The amount of concepts and learning material covered within the 5 weeks really suprised me.

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

I initially found git and git hub difficult but now I feel I have mastered it however I still struggle with javascript.

Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development bootcamp course?

I believe it is important to have the good understanding of the state you are in and also know about you wellbeing. The core work helps to build this in a individual which then can postively contribute to a better learning experience.

Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practicing programming instead? Justify your answer.

It was good to learn new things but I believe it could be cut down a little bit so we have more time to focus on programming. But the core content was really good.

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

I would like to become a proficent full stack developer working on many different aspects within the software industry. I would also like to start my own software developement company.

What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

My strengths will be my passion and my knowledge in databasess but my weakness will be trying to understand react and building on JS.

What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

My biggest non-technical challenge will be manageing my workload.

What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at EDA?

I would like to be able to develope the skill of time management and becoming accountable.

What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp

My expectations are to be better at manageing my fustrations and progress through the course with good understandings of the concepts being taught.
